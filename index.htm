HELLDIVERS 2

HELLDIVERS 2 is a 2024 third-person shooter by Sony Interactive Entertainment with the main goal of spreading democracy throughout the universe.

Factions

In HELLDIVERS 2 you play as a soldier known as a Helldiver, hence the name. There are 4 factions:

  • Terminids
  • Automatons
  • Illuminate
  • Super Earth
You and your fellow Helldivers being part of the latter; Super Earth. The other three are the enemies of Super Earth. Each faction has their own goals and mechanics.

The Terminids

The Terminids are invertebrate arthropod fiends, resembling common bugs and insects. They vary in size, from small, ankle-biting nuisances to towering and heavily armored foes. They spawn from "bug breaches", which are basically just large colony dens. To destroy them, simply throw an explosive into them, or attack them with a heavy-armor piercing weapon. Learn more about the different types of Terminids here.

The Automatons

The Automatons are, well, automatons. There are many types of them, ranging from simple bipedal robots to cyborgs to entire tanks. They spawn from factories, which can be destroyed the same way as a bug breach: simply throw an explosive into the air vents at the top, or attack them with a heavy-armor piercing weapon. There is also a type of Automaton known as the Factory Strider, which is basically a walking factory. Learn more about the different types of Automatons here.

The Illuminate

The Illuminate isn't exactly a faction, as it acts as more of a hivemind. The Voteless (the most common Illuminate) resemble a fallen citizen of Super Earth, but mutated into a horrific zombie-like creature. They spawn from Illuminate dropships, which--unlike the other two factions--have a shield you have to break before using explosives or other munitions. Learn more about the different types of Illuminate here.
